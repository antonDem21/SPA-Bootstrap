ABOUT THE ARTIST

Marilee Lee

NY native with a BA in Art Education, she began participating in juried shows and hanging in galleries in 1977. Relocating to Jensen Beach in 1997. A previous exhibiting director and VP of the AAMC until she opened the Artist FRAMING Galley in 2004. She now rotates her present work in her home gallery.
